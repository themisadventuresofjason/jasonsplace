STARBALLZ

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Starballz is a very "interesting" movie that first got released into the public at

somepoint after June 27, 2001. The general idea of the film is basically a hentai parody of

Starwars featuring a variety of characters from different anime.

Personally I felt the movie was shitty but in a good way, and no im NOT giving it a mild thumbs up

because of titties bouncing, I sorta like this film because it just is pretty funny to me. I liked some of

the stupid one-liners in it and other aspects of it aswell but those are the only redeeming parts of the movie.

My problems come in with the animation which is mostly good but sometimes it looks a bit goofy or is just a

bunch of low effort loops and shit and the music comes off as kinda bad sometimes but it does sound good in

some certain parts.

In conclusion, I give this "masterpiece" a 4/10 personally.
